About JAMI ENERGY LTD

Leading the way in industrial and structural engineering solutions since 2011

Our Story

JAMI ENERGY LIMITED was established on March 14, 2011, with a vision to become Nigeria's premier provider of industrial and structural engineering solutions. Founded as a Private Unlimited Company under registration number RC 941780, we have grown from a small engineering consultancy to a comprehensive service provider in the industrial sector.

Over the past decade, we have built our reputation on delivering high-quality engineering solutions that meet international standards while addressing the unique challenges of the Nigerian industrial landscape. Our expertise spans across consultation, engineering design, construction, procurement, and specialized metal fabrication services.

Today, JAMI ENERGY LIMITED stands as a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable, innovative, and cost-effective engineering solutions. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has enabled us to build lasting relationships with clients across various industries.

Our Mission

To provide world-class engineering consultation, construction, and fabrication services that exceed client expectations while contributing to Nigeria's industrial development through innovative and sustainable solutions.

Our Vision

To be the leading industrial and structural engineering company in Nigeria, recognized for our technical excellence, innovative solutions, and commitment to building a stronger industrial infrastructure for the nation.

Our Values

  • Excellence in every project
  • Integrity and transparency
  • Innovation and continuous improvement
  • Safety and quality assurance
  • Customer satisfaction
